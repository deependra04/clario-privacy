Privacy Policy for Clario Chrome Extension

Last Updated: August 2025

Introduction

Clario ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Clario Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Data Collected by the Extension

Clario collects the following types of information:

  1. Prompt Content: When you use the "Improve Prompt" feature, Clario accesses the text content of your prompts on supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, etc.) to enhance them.
  2. User Activity: Clario tracks your interactions with the extension, including:
    • Clicks on the "Improve Prompt" button
    • Usage of templates and history features
    • API configuration settings
  3. Settings and Preferences:
    • Your API configuration (API keys are stored locally on your device)
    • Custom templates you create
    • Extension settings and preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Prompt Enhancement: To improve and enhance your AI prompts
  • Extension Functionality: To provide the core features of Clario
  • User Experience: To save your preferences and provide personalized features
  • History and Templates: To maintain your prompt history and custom templates

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This includes:

  • Prompt history
  • Custom templates
  • API configuration
  • User preferences

API Keys

Your API keys are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Data Transmission

The only external data transmission occurs when:

  • You use the "Improve Prompt" feature (your prompt is sent to Google's Generative AI API for enhancement)
  • You test your API connection

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your personal information with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to prompt enhancement
  • Collect or store your API keys on our servers
  • Track your browsing history outside of AI platforms

We Do:

  • Use Google's Generative AI API for prompt enhancement (your prompt content is sent to Google's servers as required for the service)

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View your prompt history through the extension popup
  • Delete individual history entries
  • Clear all stored data through Chrome's extension settings
  • Disable the extension at any time

Data Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device and will persist until you:

  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear your browser data
  • Manually delete specific entries

Children's Privacy

Clario is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the extension
